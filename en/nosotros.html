ABOUT US

An audiovisual production company focused on creating images with intention, identity, and narrative. At WACH we develop audiovisual pieces designed to connect brands, ideas, and people through a contemporary visual perspective.

How the production company started

WACH was founded with the intention of building a production company capable of combining aesthetic sensitivity, technical execution, and a close, collaborative approach to every project.

From the very beginning, the goal was clear: to create visual pieces with strong character, taking care of both creative direction and every detail of production, filming, and post-production.

Today we work on projects ranging from branded content and campaigns to institutional pieces and productions with a strong and consistent visual identity.
